About Me

Hello! My name is EDMAR M. BESTUDIO, a second-year Bachelor of Science in Information Systems student. I am highly interested in system analysis, research, and technical documentation, particularly in how technology can be used to improve business processes and organizational workflows.

As a student, I enjoy working on projects that involve data gathering, structured documentation, and system development. I continuously enhance my skills in Microsoft Office tools, basic programming, web development, and database management. I take pride in producing outputs that are organized, accurate, and aligned with professional standards, especially in academic and research-based work.

Throughout my college journey, I have developed systems such as a Customer Feedback System and a Salon Reservation System. These experiences strengthened my problem-solving abilities, attention to detail, and understanding of how systems are designed, analyzed, and implemented in real-world scenarios.

I also gained valuable experience as an Office Assistant under the Cash for Work Program at the VPAA Research Office of Davao del Norte State College. In this role, I assisted in handling research documents, organizing files, and supporting daily office operations—enhancing both my communication and organizational skills.

As a consistent Dean’s Lister, I demonstrate dedication, discipline, and commitment to academic excellence. Beyond academics, I engage in creative activities and self-improvement to maintain balance and continuous growth.

My goal is to further develop my expertise in Information Systems, especially in system analysis, documentation, and process improvement. In the future, I aim to contribute to impactful projects that help organizations become more efficient through innovative and well-designed technological solutions.
